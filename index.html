HubEducation is a robust online learning platform that bridges the gap between students and instructors. It empowers students to access educational materials in PDF format published by their instructors. Instructors, in turn, can effortlessly publish and manage course materials for their students. Hosted on Digital Ocean, with nginx serving as a reliable proxy on the droplet, HubEducation ensures a seamless and secure learning experience for both students and instructors. The platform is designed to enhance the educational journey by providing a centralized hub for resource sharing and learning materials.
